Budget

Setting up a budget is one of the most important things you can do as a young adult just starting out in the working world. It will help you keep track of your income and expenses, and ensure that you're not overspending.

Here are some tips for setting up a budget:

  • List all of your income sources.
  • Track your expenses for a month to see where your money is going.
  • Identify areas where you can cut back on spending.
  • Allocate a portion of your income towards savings.
